My Music History

My life has been filled with the beauty of music. Since I was three years old until today, I have played in concerts, high school band and orchestra, and local clubs around the Tacoma area. Music is everywhere in the world, and penetrates our very souls. That is why I have dedicated so much of my life to playing it. My hope is that you will find the beauty for yourself as you look over this site.

Christoffer Hemming
